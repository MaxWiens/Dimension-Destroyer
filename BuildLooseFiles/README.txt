​After stealing the gun that can kill reality, you have been ambushed by the Interdimensional Police.
Defeat them and erase this reality to hide the traces of your crimes.​

To run:
Launch "Dimension Destroyers.exe".

## Keyboard and Mouse Controls

WASD - Movement
Mouse - Look
Space - Jump
Left Mouse - Fire
Scroll Wheel - Change Weapon
Escape - Pause

​## Gamepad Controls

Left Stick - Movement
Right Stick - Look
South Button - Jump
Right Trigger - Fire
Shoulder Buttons - Change Weapon
Start - Pause
